Cryptography

Cryptography is essential for national security in the cyber world. It involves the use of mathematical algorithms to encrypt and decrypt sensitive information, ensuring that only authorized parties can access it. This is crucial for protecting government communications, military operations, and critical infrastructure from cyber threats. Through computer science advancements, modern cryptographic techniques such as public key encryption and digital signatures provide robust security measures that safeguard data integrity and confidentiality. As cyber threats continue to evolve, cryptography remains a vital tool in defending national security interests.
