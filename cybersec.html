Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is essential to both national and economical security. The digital space has become the new battleground for intelligence collection, surveillance and stealth attacks which may go undetected for long periods of time.

Since the start of the war, Russia has carried out cyberattacks on Ukraine, targeting critical infrastructure like power, communications and logistics to degrade its abilty to function. The Russians used disruption techniques like malware and DDoS attacks in its operations against Ukraine.

Cyber security can help prevent such attacks from crippling a nation's capabilities, especially during war.

  • Confidentiality, Integrity, Availablilty (CIA triad) are core security properties used to protect data from unauthorized access, tampering and outages.
  • Real-time threat-intel sharing of indicators of compormise and adversary tactics with intelliegence partners who often work together on national security issues
  • Incident response for detection, forensics and containment of an attack and to restore operations
