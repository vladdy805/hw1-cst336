Cybersecurity

Computer science is the what makes cybersecurity possible. It allows us to create tools and systems that not only protect sensitve information but also that prevent cyber attacks on national infrastructure. Through advanced algorithms and machine learning, computer science enables the detection and response to cyber threats in real-time, enhancing national security.

Concepts like encryption, firewalls, and intrusion detection systems are used to safeguard data and maintain the integrity of critical systems. Network security protocols, developed through computer science, ensure secure communication channels, protecting against eavesdropping and data breaches.
