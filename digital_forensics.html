Digital Forensics

Digital forensics is a critical component of national security in the cyber world. It involves the investigation and analysis of digital devices to uncover evidence of cybercrimes and security breaches. Through computer science techniques, digital forensics experts can recover deleted files, analyze network traffic, and trace cyber attacks back to their source. This information is vital for prosecuting cybercriminals and preventing future attacks, thereby enhancing national security.
