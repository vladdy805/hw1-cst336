National Security in the Cyber World

National security is about protecting a nation's people, institutions and infrastructure. With the rise of technology, national security now extends into the cyber world.

Cybersecurity is crucial for prevention, dectection, and response to cyber threats that can compromise national security.

Cryptography plays a vital role in securing communications and sensitive data, ensuring confidentiality and integrity.

Digital forensics helps investigate cyber incidents, gather evidence, and support legal actions against cybercriminals. In this site, we explore how computer science contributes to national security
